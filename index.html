üî® Step-by-Step Assembly

‚è±Ô∏è Time Required: 30-45 minutes with 2 people
1 Position Base Platform

Place wooden base on level ground, laminated flooring facing up. Orient with open front toward desired direction.

2 Unfold Wall Panels

The three walls are hinged together and fold flat for transport.

  • Lay folded wall assembly flat on base
  • Pine back wall is in the middle
  • Aluminum side walls fold outward via piano hinges
  • Person 1 holds back wall stable
  • Person 2 unfolds each side wall to 90¬∞

3 Lock Corners with Cam Locks

Secure each corner where back wall meets side walls.

  • Align pre-drilled holes at each corner (top & bottom)
  • Insert cam bolt through holes in both panels
  • Insert Allen key into cam mechanism
  • Rotate 90¬∞ clockwise until tight
  • Repeat for all 4-6 cam locks
  • Test rigidity - panels should not move
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Ensure panels are at exact 90¬∞ angle before locking. Use a square or measure diagonal distances to verify.
4 Secure Walls to Base

Attach wall assembly to wooden base platform using brackets or bolts at the bottom of each wall panel.

  • Use spirit level to check walls are vertical
  • Tighten all base connections firmly
5 Install Roof Panel

REQUIRES 2 PEOPLE - Roof is heavy and awkward to handle.

  • Both people lift pine roof panel from opposite sides
  • Ensure bee statue faces forward (open front)
  • Position roof on top of all three walls
  • Adjust for even overhang on all sides
  • Attach roof clips around perimeter:
    • 2-3 clips on back wall edge
    • 2 clips on each side wall edge
    • Hook clips over wall frames and under roof edge
    • Tighten or lock each clip
  • Verify roof is stable and won't shift
6 Anchor Structure

Prevent movement and tipping.

  • Outdoor/Grass: Hammer ground stakes through base corners into earth
  • Indoor/Hard Surface: Place sandbags or water weights at base corners
  • Test stability by gently pushing structure
7 Position Display Items
  • Place display tables inside structure
  • Position honeycomb flower display
  • Arrange for good customer flow
8 Final Safety Check

‚úÖ Checklist before opening:

  • All cam locks fully engaged and tight
  • Roof clips secure on all sides
  • Structure doesn't wobble when pushed
  • Base is level and stable
  • No sharp edges exposed
  • Perspex panels clean and intact
  • Ground stakes fully inserted (if outdoor)
